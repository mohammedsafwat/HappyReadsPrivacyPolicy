# Privacy Policy for HappyReads
**Last updated:** 2025-02-24

HappyReads (“the App”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

---

## 1. Data Collection
HappyReads **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data**.  
All information you enter in the App (such as your reading list and progress) is stored **locally on your device only**.

No data is sent to any servers, third parties, or external services.

---

## 2. Data Storage
All app data remains on your device.  
If you delete the App, all stored data is permanently deleted.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services
HappyReads does **not** use:

- Analytics tools  
- Advertising SDKs  
- Tracking technologies  
- Crash reporting services  
- External databases  

The App operates fully offline.

---

## 4. Children’s Privacy
Since the App does not collect or transmit any data, it is safe for users of all ages.

---

## 5. Changes to This Policy
If HappyReads adds features that require data collection in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
